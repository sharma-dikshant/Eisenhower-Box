    Task Management Instructions

    • Enter up to 4 tasks and assign each a priority.
    • Tasks are automatically sorted by priority.
    • Mark the checkbox for a completed task and click the "Update" button to refresh the list and remove completed tasks.
    • Click the "Clear All" button to remove all tasks from the list.

    About the App

    • Efficient Task Management: Manage up to 4 tasks daily with assigned priorities.
    • Dynamic Updates: Easily mark tasks as completed and update the list in real-time.
    • Prioritized Sorting: Tasks are automatically organized based on their priority levels.
    • Simple Controls: Clear all tasks with a single click using the "Clear All" button.

    Tips for Effective Task Management

    • Prioritize Wisely: Assign higher priority to tasks that are most important and urgent.
    • Stay Focused: Concentrate on one task at a time to enhance productivity.
    • Review Regularly: At the end of the day, review your task list and carry over unfinished tasks.
    • Set Realistic Goals: Keep your daily task list manageable to avoid feeling overwhelmed.
    • Use Downtime Efficiently: Utilize any free time to prepare for upcoming tasks.
